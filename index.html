Software Developer

Thanks to my habit of signing up for mailing lists, I stumbled upon Skillcrush, an online school that teaches tech skills. After the Web Developer course, I was hooked. I learned that the World Wide Web isn't just maintained by men in basements. I can join the party, too! I've spent the last year at Ada Developers Academy training to be a developer and using those skills and learning more at an internship at EMC Isilon in Seattle.

What I love about being a software developer is creating solutions to problems through code. And if I can do that on a collaborative team? Even better. Helloooo World.
